 The music industry is dirty. So dirty that even if you're in the top of your game, you still might need to show up to the CEO of your record label's house with a dagger in hand and your demo CD and stab it to a tree in order to send a message to that person to drop you from their label so that you can have the rights to your music back. That's what absolute icon Bonnie McKee had to do in order to get herself released from the label so that she could re-record her music and give it to the world almost 10 years after it was shelved unfairly.
